# Customer Success Maturity Assessment

This assessment helps us understand your organization's current Customer Success practices across five dimensions. Your responses will inform a tailored recommendations report.

## How to Approach This

Be specific where you can — names of tools, approximate numbers, concrete examples. Vague answers produce vague recommendations. If a question doesn't apply to your organization, explain why rather than skipping it.

Expected time: approximately 30 minutes.

## Section 1: Strategy and Leadership

**1.1** How does your organization define Customer Success? Is there a formal charter, mission statement, or mandate — and if so, who owns it?

**1.2** Where does Customer Success report in your org structure? Describe the reporting line from individual CSMs up to the executive sponsor.

**1.3** How are Customer Success goals aligned with company-level objectives? Give an example of a CS goal that directly maps to a business outcome.

## Section 2: Customer Journey

**2.1** Describe your customer journey from signed contract through renewal. What are the defined stages, and what triggers the transition between them?

**2.2** How do you identify customers who are at risk of churning? What signals or data points does your team monitor?

**2.3** What does your onboarding process look like for a new customer? Walk us through the first 90 days, including who's involved and what milestones you track.

**2.4** How do you define and measure "first value" for your customers? Is this consistent across segments or does it vary?

## Section 3: Team and Operations

**3.1** Describe your CS team structure. Include roles, specializations, headcount, and how accounts are assigned (by segment, region, industry, or other criteria).

**3.2** What is your current CSM-to-account ratio? How does this vary across customer segments (enterprise, mid-market, SMB)?

**3.3** What tools and systems does your CS team use daily? Include your CS platform, CRM, communication tools, and any internal systems.

**3.4** How do you handle the handoff from Sales to CS? What information transfers, and where does context commonly get lost?

## Section 4: Measurement and Data

**4.1** What metrics does your CS team track? List both leading indicators (health scores, adoption metrics, engagement) and lagging indicators (churn, NRR, expansion).

**4.2** How do you currently calculate customer health? Describe the inputs, weighting, and how the score is used in practice.

**4.3** What data quality challenges does your team face? Where are the gaps between what you'd like to measure and what you can actually track?

## Section 5: Growth and Expansion

**5.1** Does your CS team have expansion or upsell responsibilities? If so, how are targets set and how does the handoff to Sales work?

**5.2** What does your renewal process look like? How far in advance does it start, and what's the typical workflow?

**5.3** Where do you see the biggest opportunity to improve your Customer Success function in the next 12 months?
