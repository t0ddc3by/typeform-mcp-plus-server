# Product Feedback Survey

We'd love to hear about your experience with our product. This short survey takes about 5 minutes to complete.

## How to Approach This

Answer each question based on your actual experience — we value candid feedback over polished responses. If a question doesn't apply, say so and explain why.

## Section 1: Overall Experience

**1.1** How would you describe your overall experience with our product so far? What's working well and what isn't?

**1.2** What was the primary problem you were trying to solve when you started using our product? How well is it solving that problem today?

## Section 2: Onboarding and Support

**2.1** Walk us through your onboarding experience. What went smoothly and where did you get stuck?

**2.2** How responsive and helpful has our support team been when you've needed assistance?

## Section 3: Looking Ahead

**3.1** If you could change one thing about the product, what would it be and why?

**3.2** Would you recommend our product to a colleague? What would you tell them?

If you have screenshots, documents, or other files that illustrate your feedback, please upload them below.
